Introduction

This tool is designed for people who have performed small scale nanopore sequencing on barcoded sequences. It allows you to examine the barcodes which are present and then split your file into multiple output files based on the presence of barcodes at the end of the seuqences.

All you need to have to use the tools is

  1. The small scale nanopore file you recevied from your sequencing service
  2. The length (number of bases) of the barcode(s) on your sequences
  3. Whether you have barcodes at both ends of your sequence, or just one.
  4. [Optional] the specific list of barcodes you used along with a name for the associated sample

The program will aim to identify putative barcode sequences and combinations and you can filter and validate these before creating a split version of your file.

Data submitted to this site are not made available other than via the random URL generated when you submit your data. All data are deleted from our systems one week after they were submitted.
